Our Advantages

Why Choose Us

Wake up to serene mornings embraced by lush greenery, where tranquility meets the gentle sounds of nature. Our resort offers convenient access from the main highway, making your journey smooth and effortless. The interiors are thoughtfully designed using bamboo craftsmanship, creating a unique blend of traditional charm and modern comfort.

Guests can enjoy a diverse range of multi-cuisine dining options, perfect for family gatherings and friendly get-togethers. Located close to popular tourist destinations, tea plantations, and adventure spots, our resort is an ideal base for exploring the beauty and excitement of Wayanad.
